PARTICIPATION REGISTRATION & MENTOR APPLICATIONS TIMELINE

Participant Registration
Participant registration for freshmen and sophomores are open now. The priority deadline is Sunday, September 8, 2024. Students applying by the priority deadline will receive first consideration for the limited spots available for Fall Retreat.

The final application deadline is Sunday, September 22, 2024. The program fee is $150.

Financial assistance is available for the program fee and for the cost of transportation to and from the program for all students.
The program fee is non-refundable after Sunday, September 29, 2024.

Mentor Application & Timeline
 Seniors and young professionals based in the Southeast, Midwest, Northeast, and Florida regions are invited to apply as mentors to the program. Mentors are an integral part of the Freshman Retreat experience as they are responsible for delivering content, providing students with advice and guidance, supporting program operations, and maintaining a safe environment for all at Freshman Retreat. Mentors at Freshman Retreat will be able to meet fellow young professionals, build leadership skills, and help shape the next generation of Ismaili students.

Mentor applications are due no later than 11:59 PM CT on Sunday, September 8, 2024. Mentors will be interviewed on a rolling basis as applications are received. Mentors will receive an update on their application by Sunday, September 15. The mentor program fee is $150 and financial assistance is available to cover the program fee and transportation expenses.

Mentors are expected to commit to three virtual training (up to 90 minutes each) in the weeks leading into the program, likely on weeknights. In addition, mentors are required to arrive at the site by 11am on Friday, October 11 for a site walkthrough and an on-site training.
